Drop Your Video to Start Editing — Free

MP4, MOV, WEBM — no file size limits, no watermark, no time limit

ClipForge is a free browser-based video editor where you can trim and crop any video — regardless of file size or length — with no daily limits, no watermark, and no account required. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Trim Any Video

Set a precise start and end point using the draggable timeline handles or type in exact timestamps in min:sec format. Preview your trim before exporting.

Crop to Any Ratio

Use the interactive crop overlay to drag and resize your frame. Choose from preset aspect ratios — 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for Instagram, 9:16 for TikTok & Reels.

Export as MP4

Choose Low, Medium, or High quality and export a clean H.264 MP4 file with no watermark. Processing happens entirely in your browser — your file never leaves your device.

100% Private

No file is ever uploaded to a server. ClipForge uses ffmpeg.wasm — a WebAssembly build of the industry-standard FFmpeg encoder — running entirely inside your browser tab.

No File Size Limits

Unlike cloud-based editors that restrict uploads to a few hundred MB, ClipForge handles files as large as your browser's available memory — perfect for long, high-resolution videos.

No Daily Limits

Most free video editors cap you at a few exports per day. ClipForge has zero usage limits — trim and crop as many videos as you want, as often as you want, for free.

About ClipForge

ClipForge is a free, open, browser-based video editor. It requires no account, no installation, and transmits no data to any server. All video processing is powered by ffmpeg.wasm running entirely inside your browser.

  • Trim videos to precise start and end points.
  • Crop with interactive overlay and aspect ratio presets (16:9, 1:1, 9:16).
  • Export to H.264 MP4 with low, medium, or high quality presets.
  • Works with MP4, MOV, and WEBM input formats.
  • Fully responsive — works on desktop and mobile browsers.
  • Deployable on Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, or any static host.
